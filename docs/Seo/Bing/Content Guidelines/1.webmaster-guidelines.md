                the crawler can or cannot
                access on your site. Robots.txt is primarily used to instruct
                and manage crawler traffic, For example
                you can tell Bingbot not to crawl less helpful content such as
                search result pages or login pages.
                <ul class="marginZero">
                    <li>Place the robots.txt in your root directory (the topmost
                        directory) of your website. Do not
                        place it in a subdirectory. </li>
                    <li>Blocking Bing from crawling a page will likely remove
                        the page from the index. However, using
                        Disallow does not guarantee that a page will not appear
                        within the index or search results. If
                        you would like to block a specific page from getting
                        crawled or indexed, you should use the
                        noindex robots meta tag instead of disallowing it in the
                        robots.txt. </li>
                    <li>Review your robots.txt often to ensure it's up to date. Review URLs disallowed
                        by robots.txt in Bing Webmaster Tools to
                        ensure it remains accurate. </li>
                    <li><a href="https://www.bing.com/webmasters/help/how-to-create-a-robots-txt-file-cb7c31ec">Learn more by reading how
                            to create a
                            robots.txt text file</a><br><br> </li>
                </ul>
            </li>
            <li><a class="bookmark" id="save_resources"><b>Save resources:</b></a>
                Use <a href="https://blogs.bing.com/webmaster/2008/02/12/announcing-crawler-improvements-for-live-search/">
                    HTTP Compression and Conditional Get</a> to reduce
                bandwidth used by the crawlers and your
                customers while improving page load speed. </li>
        </ul>

        <a class="bookmark" id="abuse_and_examples_of_things_to_avoid">
            <h1>滥用和需要避免的事项示例</h1>
        </a>

        <p>搜索引擎优化是一种合法做法，旨在改善网站的技术和内容方面，使内容更易于查找、更相关且更容易被搜索引擎爬虫访问。大多数 SEO 做法会使网站对 Bing 更具吸引力；然而，执行 SEO 相关工作并不保证排名会提高或网站会从 Bing 获得更多流量。此外，如果过度使用，某些 SEO 做法可能被滥用，导致搜索引擎的惩罚。<br></p>

        <p>这些 Bing 网站管理员指南只描述了一些最普遍的不当、操纵性或误导性行为形式。参与滥用行为（如下面概述的做法）的网站被认为是低质量的。因此，这些网站可能会遭受排名惩罚，网站标记被忽略，并且可能不会被选择进行索引。Microsoft 可能会对您的网站采取任何不当或欺骗性做法的行动，即使是这里没有描述的那些。如果您觉得您的网站受到了行动，您可以通过 Bing 网站管理员工具联系我们的支持团队。此外，用户可以在执行重现问题的搜索后，使用 bing.com 页脚中的反馈链接报告任何这些做法的滥用。这里可以看到一个例子。<br></p>
        
        <blockquote><img border="0" width="601" height="383" id="Picture 1" src="/webmaster/help/helpimage/AbuseFeedbackExample" alt="Abuse Feedback Example"></blockquote>
        <ul>
            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="cloaking">隐藏真实内容：</a></b>
                隐藏真实内容是指向像 Bingbot 这样的搜索爬虫显示网页的一个版本，而向普通访问者显示另一个版本的做法。向用户显示与爬虫看到的内容不同的内容可能被视为垃圾邮件策略，并可能对您网站的排名造成损害，导致您的网站从 Bing 索引中被除名。网站应该对向爬虫和普通访问者不同响应极其谨慎，不应将隐藏真实内容作为原则。<br><br></li>
            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="link_schemes_buying_and_spamming">链接计划、链接购买、链接垃圾邮件：</a></b>
                虽然链接计划可能成功增加指向您网站的链接数量，但它们无法为您的网站带来高质量的链接，使您的网站没有获得正面收益。操纵入站链接以人为地增加指向网站的链接数量可能导致您的网站从 Bing 索引中被除名。<br><br></li>
            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="social_media_schemes">Social media schemes:</a></b> 
                Social media schemes are similar to link farms in that they seek to artificially exploit a network effect to game Bing’s algorithm. The reality is that social media schemes are easy to see in action, and the website’s value is deprecated. One such example is auto follow. Auto follows encourage follower growth on social sites such as Twitter, and work by automatically following anyone who follows you. Over time this creates a scenario where the number of followers you have is more or less the same as the number of people following you. This does not indicate you have a strong influence. Following few people while having a high follower count would indicate a stronger influential voice.<br><br></li>
            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="duplicate_content">Duplicate content:</a></b>
                Duplicating
                content across
                multiple URLs can lead to Bing losing
                trust in some of those URLs over time.
                This issue should be
                managed by fixing the root cause of the
                problem. The <span class="notranslate">rel=canonical
                </span>
                element can also be used but should be
                considered a secondary solution to 
                fixing the core problem.
                If excessive parameterization is causing the
                duplicate content issue, we encourage
                you to use the <a href="https://www.bing.com/webmasters/help/URL-Inspection-55a30305">URL Inspection tool</a>. 
                <br><br>
            </li>

            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="scraped_content">Scraped
                        Content:</a></b> Scraping or
                copying content from
                other more reputable websites may not
                add value to your users and may be
                considered copyright
                infringement. Instead of republishing
                content from other sites without adding
                any original content or
                value, you should create original
                content differentiating your
                website. Slightly modifying and
                republishing content from other websites
                also constitutes scraping. <br><br></li>
            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="keyword_stuffing_or_landing_pages_with_irrelevant_keywords">
                        关键词填充或在页面中添加不相关关键词：</a></b>
                        创建内容时，请确保为真实用户和读者创建内容，而不是为了诱使搜索引擎更好地排名您的内容。仅仅为了人为地提高特定搜索词排名概率而在内容中填充特定关键词违反了我们的指导原则。这可能导致您的网站在我们的搜索结果中被降级甚至除名。<br><br></li>

            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="automatically_generated_content">
                        Automatically generated content:
                    </a></b>
                    Machine-generated content is information that is generated by an automated computer process, application, or other mechanisms without any active intervention of a human. Content like this is considered malicious and usually contains garbage text only created to garnish a higher ranking. This type of content will result in penalties.<br><br></li>
            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="affiliate_programs_without_adding_sufficient_value">
                        Affiliate programs
                        without adding sufficient value:
                    </a></b>
                    Websites that link products from other websites (such as Amazon, eBay, etc.) but pretend that they are an official retailer or in affiliation with those sites are called Thin Affiliation sites, and they add little to no incremental value (like extra reviews, search functionality, Editor’s Choice, etc.). and are only intended to take credit for other content. It can lead to demotion or even delisting your website from our search results.<br><br></li>

            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="malicious_behavior">
                        Malicious behavior:</a></b>
                        When creating content and managing your site, make sure to not participate in phishing or installing viruses, trojans, or other badware by verifying your content, maintaining your Content Management System, keeping your operating system up to date, and limiting access to who can publish on your site. Malicious behavior can lead to demotion or even delisting your website from our search results.<br><br>
            </li>

            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="misleading_structured_data_markup">
                        Misleading structured data
                        markup:
                    </a></b> 
                    Site with markups must be accurate and representative of the page that the tags are on. Sites must not have markup which is irrelevant to the page and is inaccurate or misleading. <br> <br>
            </li>

            <li><b><a class="bookmark" id="prompt_injection">
                Prompt injection:
                </a></b> 
                Do not add content on your webpages which attempts to perform prompt injection attacks on language models used by Bing. This can lead to demotion or even delisting of your website from our search results. 
            </li>
        </ul>
