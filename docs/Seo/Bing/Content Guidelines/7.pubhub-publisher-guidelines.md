    <h2>Prohibited content</h2>
    <div>
        <p>Prohibited content includes, but is not limited to, the following:</p>
        <ul>
            <li><b>Threatening or violent activity.</b> We do not allow content that promotes illegal
                activities or is used to threaten, harass, organize violence, or support violent organizations.
            </li>
            <li><b>Promotion of hate.</b> We do not allow content that condones hate or incites
                violence
                based on race, gender, ethnicity, religion, or sexual preference.</li>
            <li><b>Sexually explicit content.</b> We do not allow content that is sexually explicit or
                promotes illegal sexual activity, or that is sexually suggestive in nature.</li>
            <li><b>Illegal drug use.</b> We do not allow gratuitous references to or
                images of illegal drug use and paraphernalia.</li>
        </ul>
    </div>
