<h3><strong>Testing locations</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>CovidTestingFacility</strong></h4>
<p> If your page has information about risk assessments and testing centers for the COVID-19 pandemic, you can include a
	SpecialAnnouncement structured data element with a gettingTestedInfo property and a CovidTestingFacility within the
	about property. This allows Bing to consider including the information from your page, when users are seeking
	information on how to get assessed to see whether getting tested is recommended and, if so, how to locate a nearby
	testing facility and find instructions for getting tested at that center.

</p>
<p>When this markup is used it is important that the underlying page:</p>
<ol>
	<li>Is the official site for a well-known healthcare facility or government health agency.</li>
	<li>Includes information on how people should be assessed to see whether getting tested is recommended.</li>
	<li>Is owned by a healthcare provider and includes information of URLs and facility locations associated with the
		service. Listing of other providers facilities is not supported at this time. </li>
</ol>

<h3><strong>Travel restrictions &amp; guidelines</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>travelBans and publicTransportClosuresInfo</strong></h4>
<p> If your page has announcements about travel restrictions, such as updated hours for transportation, closures, and
	guidelines for travel related to the spread of COVID-19, you can include a SpecialAnnouncement structured data
	element with a travelBans or publicTransportClosuresInfo property. This allows Bing to consider including the
	information on travel restrictions from your page on travel-related search results.</p>
<p>When this markup is used it is important that the underlying page:</p>
<ol>
	<li>Is an official site for a well-known government agency, travel agency, airline, hotel, or other travel
		providers.</li>
	<li>Contains the name of the special announcement at an easily identifiable and visible location.</li>
	<li>Includes the date when the restrictions information was updated, which matches the datePosted property in the
		special announcement.</li>
</ol>
